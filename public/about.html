About Us

Plant Identification Online is a free AI-powered website that helps users identify plants simply by uploading a photo.

Our mission is to make plant identification simple, fast, and accessible for students, researchers, gardeners, and nature enthusiasts around the world.

Version 2 focuses on providing accurate plant identification and description of a plant with a clean, user-friendly experience, deployed on Cloudflare Workers for fast global performance.
